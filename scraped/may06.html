Cal Squash Box League - May 2006

Rules

Announcements:

  • Please schedule and play all your matches by May 31st 2006. Last month's results here.
  • Spring Quarter Dues: $20 for boxes March through May-- Spring dues are now being accepted. Failure to pay dues will result in you being dropped from the league.
  • Payments can be made by check payable "UC Regents- Cal Squash Club" and deposited into the slit in the lockbox on Court 1 (marked Cal Squash Box League Deposit) or mailed to: Cal Squash Club, 2301 Bancroft Way, Berkeley, CA 94720.
  • After a match, record your match points on the board on Court 1. Remember:
    3/0 = 6 points to the winner, 1 point to the loser.
    3/1 = 5 points to the winner, 2 points to the loser.
    3/2 = 4 points to the winner, 3 points to the loser.
    No show or walk over (injuries) = 5 points to the winner, 0 points to the loser.
  • Any further questions can be directed to boxleague at corimbia dot com.
