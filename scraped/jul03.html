Cal Squash Box League - July 2003

Rules

Announcements:

  • Please schedule and play all your matches by Friday, August 1, 2003.
  • After a match, record your match points on the board on Court 1. Remember:
    3/0 = 6 points to the winner, 1 point to the loser.
    3/1 = 5 points to the winner, 2 points to the loser.
    3/2 = 4 points to the winner, 3 points to the loser.
    No show or walk over (injuries) = 5 points to the winner, 0 points to the loser.
  • Don't forget to sign up online for the August Box League by Friday, August 1, 2003.
    The top players will be eligible for our two East Bay Squash League teams!
  • Any further questions can be directed to Harlan Yu.
