Rules

Announcements:

  • Please schedule and play all your watches by July 31, 2013. Last month's results here.
  • After a match, record your match points by clicking on your name. Remember:
    3/0 = 6 points to the winner, 1 point to the loser.
    3/1 = 5 points to the winner, 2 points to the loser.
    3/2 = 4 points to the winner, 3 points to the loser.
    No show or walk over (injuries) = 5 points to the winner, 0 points to the loser.
  • Playing none of your matches will drop you from the next month's box league unless you request otherwise.
  • Any further questions can be directed to jveitchv at gmail dot com
