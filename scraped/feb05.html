Cal Squash Box League - Febuary 2005

Rules

Announcements:

  • Please schedule and play all your matches by Saturday Febuary 28th 2005.
  • Payments can be made by check payable "UC Regents- Cal Squash Club" and deposited into the slit in the lockbox on Court 1 (marked Cal Squash Box League Deposit).
  • After a match, record your match points on the board on Court 1. Remember:
    3/0 = 6 points to the winner, 1 point to the loser.
    3/1 = 5 points to the winner, 2 points to the loser.
    3/2 = 4 points to the winner, 3 points to the loser.
    No show or walk over (injuries) = 5 points to the winner, 0 points to the loser.
  • Any further questions can be directed to gjiz at yahoo dot com.
