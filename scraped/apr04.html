Cal Squash Box League - April 2004

Rules

Announcements:

  • Please schedule and play all your matches by Friday, April 30, 2004.
  • Spring Quarter Dues: $20 for boxes March through May-- if you haven't already paid, please do so ASAP.
  • Payments can be made by check payable "UC Regents- Cal Squash Club" and deposited into the wooden lockbox on Court 1 (in the slit underneath the lid, marked Cal Squash Box League).
  • After a match, record your match points on the board on Court 1. Remember:
    3/0 = 6 points to the winner, 1 point to the loser.
    3/1 = 5 points to the winner, 2 points to the loser.
    3/2 = 4 points to the winner, 3 points to the loser.
    No show or walk over (injuries) = 5 points to the winner, 0 points to the loser.
  • Any further questions can be directed to harlanyu at uclink dot berkeley dot edu.
